Welcome to the Festival

The Maluti Cultural Festival is a special tourism event that celebrates Basotho culture, traditional music, food and dance.visitors from different places come togethr to experience the unique traditions of Lesotho. The festival also provides an opportunity for tourists to enjoy local crafts, traditional clothing and Basotho cuisine while exploring the beautiful landscapes of Lesotho.
